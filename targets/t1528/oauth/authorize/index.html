“Doc Sync” wants access

Benign T1528 demo. A mock OAuth consent screen requesting high-risk scopes from an unknown app — the shape of a consent-phishing attack. The EDR detects the navigation and closes the tab. Nothing is granted; no data leaves your machine.

This app is requesting permission to:

  • Read your mail (mail.read)
  • Maintain access to data you've given it access to (offline_access)
  • Read all files you can access (files.read.all)
